A Governor for All of Wisconsin

Are you tired of politicians who seem more interested in fighting each other than in fighting for you? I know I am. I’m not a career politician. I’m a neighbor, a parent, and a small business owner who has seen the promise of Wisconsin get buried under partisan gridlock.

I’m running for Governor to change that. My pledge is simple: to be a leader for everyone, regardless of their zip code or who they voted for in the last election. This campaign is about replacing division with dialogue, restoring common sense, and ensuring your voice is heard in Madison. It’s time for a government that works for the people again.

My Platform for Wisconsin

Practical policies for a prosperous and united state.

Economic Growth & Job Creation

Championing small businesses, investing in modern manufacturing, and cutting red tape to make Wisconsin the best place to work and build a future.

Affordable Healthcare

Working to lower prescription drug costs, protect rural hospitals, and ensure every Wisconsinite has access to quality, affordable healthcare.

Education Excellence

Investing in our public schools, supporting our teachers, and expanding access to technical education to prepare our kids for the jobs of tomorrow.
